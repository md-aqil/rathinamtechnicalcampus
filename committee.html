Industry Institute Interaction Cell

  • Promotes closer interaction between the academic field and the professional field
  • Provide closer links with industries
  • Identifies the gap between need of the industry and end product of the institute
  • Organises workshops, cConferences and symposia with joint participation of the industries
  • Encourages engineers from industry to deliver lectures
  • Arranges visits of staff members to various industries

    Women Development Cell

  • Enhances understanding of women’s issues
  • Makes the college campus a safe place for women
  • Creates awareness of women’s rights and duties
  • Facilitates women’s empowerment through guest lectures, seminars, awareness programs, life skill training programs, entrepreneur training and other welfare activities
  • Promotes intellectual and cultural activities for overall personality development
  • Facilitates making of informed choices in areas like education, employment and health, with special focus on reproductive health

    Entrepreneurship Development Guidance Cell

  • A platform to interact with and link industries and educational institutions
  • Trains the students to produce entrepreneurs
  • Organises Entrepreneurship Motivation Programs, Skill development programs, Entrepreneurship Development Programs and competitions
  • Promotes innovation and create an entrepreneurial culture at Rathinam

    Career Development Cell
